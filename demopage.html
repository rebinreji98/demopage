AEM stands for Adobe Experience Manager. It is a comprehensive content management solution offered by Adobe, designed to help businesses create, manage, and deliver digital experiences across various channels, including websites, mobile apps, and forms.

AEM includes powerful DAM capabilities for managing digital assets such as images, videos, documents, and other media files. It provides features for asset organization, metadata management, versioning, and asset reuse across multiple channels.

AEM supports the management of multiple websites and digital properties from a single platform. It provides tools for creating and managing site structures, templates, and content workflows across different sites and languages.

AEM seamlessly integrates with other Adobe Marketing Cloud solutions, such as Adobe Analytics, Adobe Target, and Adobe Campaign, enabling marketers to leverage data-driven insights and orchestrate personalized marketing campaigns.
